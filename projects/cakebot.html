CakeBot

PS70 Project 11 Robotics Automation

Project Overview

CakeBot is an automated cake decorating system that combines robotics with culinary arts to create intricate designs on cakes. The project explores the intersection of technology and food preparation.

Development Process

Mechanical Design

Designed and built the robotic arm and frosting delivery system.
