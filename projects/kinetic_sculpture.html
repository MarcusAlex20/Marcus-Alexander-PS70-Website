Kinetic Sculpture

PS70 Project 03 Mechanical Design Art

Project Overview

This project involved creating a moving sculpture that demonstrates principles of motion and balance through mechanical design and artistic expression.

Design Process

Concept Development

Explored various concepts for creating motion and balance in the sculpture.

Mechanical Design

Designed the mechanical components to achieve desired motion.

Assembly & Testing

Assembled components and tested the sculpture's movement.

Results

Kinetic sculpture in motion

Sculpture design

Challenges & Solutions

Balance and Motion

Challenges in achieving smooth, continuous motion while maintaining balance.

Solutions:

  • Implemented counterweights for balance
  • Used bearings for smooth motion
  • Optimized component placement
