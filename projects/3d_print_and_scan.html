PS70 Project 05 3D Printing Scanning

Project Overview

This project involved 3D printing a giraffe model and exploring various scanning techniques, combining digital design with physical fabrication.

Process

3D Model Preparation

Prepared the giraffe model for 3D printing, including support structures and print settings.

Printing

Executed the 3D printing process with careful monitoring of print quality.

Scanning

Explored different scanning techniques to capture physical objects in digital form.

  • Enhanced post-processing methods
